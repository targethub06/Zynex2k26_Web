    Who We Are

    About the Department

    The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) at PSNA College of Engineering and Technology, Dindigul is at the forefront of computing innovation in Tamil Nadu.

    Established with a vision to produce skilled AI engineers, the department offers a comprehensive B.E program that blends theoretical foundations with hands-on project experience in cutting-edge AI/ML technologies.

    Our state-of-the-art labs, dedicated faculty, and strong industry partnerships create an environment where students don't just learn technology — they create it.

    🎯 VISION

    To be a globally recognized centre of excellence in AI & ML education, inspiring ethical and innovative technologists.

    🚀 MISSION

    Empower students with cutting-edge AI skills, foster research, and build leaders who solve real-world challenges through intelligent systems.
